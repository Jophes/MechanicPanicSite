  • What is Mechanic Panic?

    Mechanic Panic is a fun indie couch co-op designed for all ages. Working as a team of one to four players, you and your robot pals must race against the clock to repair and replace parts, paint the bodywork and fill'er up and ready to go. fix up and cash out as many cars as possible to earn cash for upgrades, unlocks and progression!
