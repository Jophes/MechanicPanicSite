  • What is Mechanic Panic?

    Mechanic Panic is a fun indie couch co-op designed for all ages. Working as a team of one to four players, you and your robot pals must race against the clock to repair and replace parts, paint the bodywork and fill'er up and ready to go. fix up and cash out as many cars as possible to earn cash for upgrades, unlocks and progression!

  • About Us

    We are a group of 3 students from the University Of Northampton. Originally the game started with 5 individuals who attended a Game Jam named Brains Eden. At this event the team was given 48 hours to develop a game based on the theme of 'Give and Take'. After this event 2 of the individuals decided to continue working on the game, so after picking up another student from the university to help out, we continue to work on the game to the state where it could be published and released to the public.
